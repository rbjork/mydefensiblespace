About Us



Our Mission:

We are a small group of tech entrepreneurs who have been committed for many years to community service and beneficial uses of technology to help society.

Our goal with MyDefensibleSpace is to promote community awareness and a spirit of working together to manage defensible space in the Wildland Urban Interface (WUI).

We hope to provide a community forum for homeowners and service providers to gather and find ways to meet the need to manage defensible space around their homes.

We will present resources and information, point to other websites and resources for methods, practices, tools and equipment.
